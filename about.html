About Me

I'm a multidisciplinary designer and planner with a background in architecture, urban and environmental planning, and a strong foundation in visualization, CAD, and GIS. Skilled in 3D tools like 3Ds Max, Blender, Lumion, and Adobe Photoshop, I specialize in creating detailed, functional, and visually compelling designs. Now transitioning into front-end web development, I'm expanding my skill set into the web world with a keen eye for usability, layout, and detail. Since I can remember, I've been passionate about technology and computers. Life, however, initially led me down the path of civil engineering and later into architecture and planning. It was during my master's degree at Politecnico di Torino that my path began to realign with my early interests. Thanks to my tutor and life mentor, Professor Andrea Pollio, I was introduced to the deeper connections between technology and urbanism. His guidance not only shaped my academic direction but also reawakened a part of me that had long been waiting to resurface.
